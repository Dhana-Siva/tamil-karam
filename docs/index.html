How It Works

1

Enable the Keyboard

Go to Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard → Tamil Karam

2

Type Your Tamil Text

Open WhatsApp, Messages or any app and type your Tamil message as usual.

3

Switch to Tamil Karam

Tap the 🌐 globe button and select Tamil Karam keyboard.

4

Tap "Fix Tamil Grammar"

Your text is corrected instantly! See before & after and undo if needed.

Features

  • ✓ Works in WhatsApp, Messages and all apps
  • ✓ AI-powered Tamil grammar correction
  • ✓ Shows before & after comparison
  • ✓ Undo button if you prefer the original
  • ✓ One tap to fix — simple and fast

Privacy Policy

What we collect: When you tap "Fix Tamil Grammar", your text is sent to our server solely to perform grammar correction.

What we don't do: We do not store your text, share it with third parties, or use it for any purpose other than grammar correction.

Device ID: An anonymous device identifier is used only to prevent abuse. It is not linked to your identity.

No account required: Tamil Karam does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
